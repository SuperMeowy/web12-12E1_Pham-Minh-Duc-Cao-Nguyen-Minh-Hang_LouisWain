Our Mission

The ФЭС Foundation is dedicated to curating works that challenge the boundaries of perception.


From Victorian eccentricity to the geometry of the subconscious, we maintain a living library of artists who saw the world through a different lens. We believe that art is not just a mirror to reality, but a door to the unseen.
